4. Карточки документов

D01

research/portal/README.md

Открыть

Навигационный документ задает границы направления portal: research area для концепции `open-ai.ru`, а не место реализации. Он связывает пять research-документов и фиксирует, что перевод в reviewed зависит от решения фаундера.

Решение
D02

ai-and-mango-integration-patterns-2026-06.md

Открыть

Research выбирает serverless-proxy для Yandex GPT и санитизированную content-collection `mango_ba_prompts` для Phase 1. Критичная граница: реальные клиентские промпты, секреты и PII не коммитятся и не уходят в LLM без маскирования.

Решение
D03

architecture-and-stack-comparison-2026-06.md

Открыть

Research рекомендует modular monolith, SSG + islands, Cloudflare Pages, Supabase с Phase 2 и serverless AI proxy. Astro лидирует по content-first профилю, но Angular и Next.js остаются допустимыми product decisions.

Решение
D04

concept-standards-comparison-2026-06.md

Открыть

Research compares eight concept-document approaches and recommends a hybrid of PRD core, Vision layer and TOGAF modularity. It keeps repository structure outside the concept itself: structure follows Hub policy after concept decisions.

Решение
D05

documentation-standards-comparison-2026-06.md

Открыть

Research concludes that no single documentation standard is enough. The recommended minimum is ADR + C4, with RFC/frontmatter inherited from the Hub and arc42-lite or Diátaxis added only when content volume justifies them.

Решение
D06

open-ai-portal-concept-rfc.md

Открыть

Main RFC synthesizes the portal package: portal as spoke, ADR + C4, SSG + islands, Cloudflare, Phase 2 Supabase, Yandex GPT through serverless-proxy and roadmap Phase 0-4. It is the primary decision entrypoint, not an implementation start.

Решение
D07

repository-structure-design-2026-06.md

Открыть

Research chooses option C: portal is a separate spoke repository plus portal-specific extension. It rejects a Hub subdirectory because the portal has production code, its own lifecycle and a communication role across projects.

Решение
D08

standards/portal-repository-structure.md

Открыть

Draft standard formalizes portal repositories as spoke projects with the spoke genome plus optional portal catalogs. It includes migration guidance and conformance checks, but remains non-mandatory until founder review.

Решение
D09

standards/webportal-concept-standard.md

Открыть

Draft standard defines a modular webportal concept structure: 6 required sections and 9 optional layers. It covers simple brochure sites through app portals while excluding landing pages, mobile apps, backend services and microservices.

Решение
D10

templates/webportal-concept-template.md

Открыть

Template provides a copyable concept skeleton aligned with the webportal concept standard. It depends on the standard decision and keeps `{{date}}`, so placeholder lifecycle remains a separate governance question.
