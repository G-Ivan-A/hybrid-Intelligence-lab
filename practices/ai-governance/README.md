# AI Governance Practices

Atomic practices derived from international AI governance sources. These nodes
are not legal advice; they are operational patterns for Hub and HTOM project
work.

| Practice | Source | Use when |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [NIST AI RMF profile loop](nist-ai-rmf-profile-loop.md) | NIST AI RMF | A project needs a repeatable risk-management profile. |
| [EU AI Act risk tiering](eu-ai-act-risk-tiering.md) | European Commission AI Act materials | A project may expose AI behavior to users or regulated contexts. |
| [ISO/IEC 42001 management loop](iso-42001-management-loop.md) | ISO/IEC 42001 | AI governance needs ownership, policies, and continual improvement. |
| [OpenAI evaluation and feedback loop](openai-evaluation-and-feedback-loop.md) | OpenAI safety practices | A team ships an AI-assisted workflow and needs evaluation before and after release. |
