# ISO/IEC 42001 Management Loop

## Source, author, link

- Source: ISO/IEC 42001:2023 overview.
- Organization: ISO.
- Link: https://www.iso.org/standard/42001

## Practice

Treat AI governance as a management system: policy, objectives, roles,
processes, checks, and continual improvement.

## Problem

AI rules decay when they live only as one-time documents without ownership or
review cadence.

## Apply In Hub

1. Define the AI policy or local governance rule.
2. Assign owner and affected artifacts.
3. Connect the rule to templates, validators, or review checklists.
4. Review after incidents, repeated PR comments, or project sync feedback.
5. Update the relevant standard, practice node, or template through a PR.

## Output

A management system loop for AI practices: define, apply, check, improve.

## Limits

Hub does not claim ISO certification. The practice borrows the management system
shape to keep governance alive.
